35 Section 1. The following amendment to the Constitution
36 of Alabama of 2022, is proposed:
37 PROPOSED AMENDMENT
38 (a) After the ratification of this amendment, no
39 additional county or municipal sales and use tax may be levied
40 in Tuscaloosa County unless the levy of the tax, including any
41 increase in an existing levy, is approved by a majority vote
42 of the qualified electors in the area where the tax will be
43 paid voting at a referendum held for that purpose on the date
44 of the next regularly scheduled general election not less than
45 three months after the enactment of the proposed levy of the
46 additional sales and use tax.
47 (b) Notice of each referendum shall be published in a
48 newspaper published in the county for three consecutive weeks,
49 or if a municipal tax increase in a newspaper published in the
50 municipality, not less than 60 days prior to the date of the
51 referendum.
52 (c) The county commission in the case of a countywide
53 tax, or the municipal governing body in the case of a
54 municipal tax, shall conduct not less than two public hearings
55 on the proposed sales and use tax increase prior to the date
56 of the referendum.

68 shall be held in accordance with Sections 284, 284.01, and 285
