4 SYNOPSIS:
5 Under existing law, the filling and distributing
6 of liquefied petroleum gas is regulated by the Alabama
7 Liquefied Petroleum Gas Board.
8 This bill would prohibit permitees of the board
9 from filling or storing LP-Gas cylinders for retail
10 sale within a certain distance of public gathering
11 places.
12
13
14 A BILL
15 TO BE ENTITLED
16 AN ACT
17
18 Relating to the Alabama Liquefied Petroleum Gas Board;
19 to add Section 9-17-111 to the Code of Alabama 1975, to
20 prohibit permitees from filling or storing LP-Gas cylinders
21 within a certain distance of public gathering places.
22 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA:
23 Section 1. Section 9-17-111 is added to the Code of
24 Alabama 1975, to read as follows:
25 ยง9-17-111
26 (a) No permittee operating a bulk facility for the
27 purpose of filling liquefied petroleum gas cylinders for
28 distribution for subsequent retail sale may fill or store
Page 1
distribution for subsequent retail sale may fill or store
SB2 INTRODUCED
29 filled liquefied petroleum gas cylinders within 3,000 feet of
30 the boundary line of adjoining real property upon which a
31 school, church, hospital, athletic field, or other place of
32 public gathering is located.
33 (b) This section shall not be construed to apply to a
34 retail facility where filled liquefied petroleum gas cylinders
35 are stored for retail sale, including as part of a liquefied
36 petroleum gas exchange program.
37 Section 2. This act shall become effective on October
